Glossary

Nightly price
The estimated fair price per night for this property.
Occupancy
The share of nights expected to be booked during the year.
Occupied nights
The estimated number of booked nights in the first year.
Gross revenue
Money earned before costs.
Operating costs
Regular running costs such as platform fees, cleaning, utilities, insurance, condo, maintenance, accounting, licensing, and IMI/property tax.
NOI
Net operating income. Gross revenue minus operating costs, before bank payments and tax.
Bank payment
The estimated mortgage payment.
Tax
Estimated income tax based on the simplified tax assumptions used by the app.
Final net
Money left after operating costs, bank payment, and tax.
Cash-on-equity return
Annual net income divided by the cash you put into the deal.
Payback years
How many years it would take to recover your invested cash if results stayed the same.
IMI
Portugal’s municipal property tax.
Closing costs
Estimated purchase costs such as taxes, registration, notary, and legal fees.
Review capture rate
The estimated share of bookings that result in guest reviews.
Likely range
A forecast range using about one model error above and below the base estimate.
Wider stress-test range
A wider downside/upside range using about 1.96 model errors.
Forecast strength
How much support the historical test results give to the forecast.
Stabilized occupancy
A scenario target for a mature listing after it has built reviews, visibility, and trust.
Occupancy ramp-up
The assumed increase in occupancy over time as the listing becomes more established.
Pricing discipline
Avoiding large price increases until occupancy and reviews show the listing can support them.
